Chairman's Message

Society of Engineering, Education and Entrepreneurship is a scientific society dedicated to the promotion of international education and university cooperation in the field of Science, Engineering and Entrepreneurship. The Purpose of the society is to conduct various conferences all over country as well as to publish journals in the area of engineering, education and entrepreneurship. The mission of the society is to promote and enhance the dialogue in education among the institutions devoted to Science, Engineering and Technology fields by Production of publications for research works, projects and innovation activities.
